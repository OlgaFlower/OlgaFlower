
## About me

iOS Engineer with 5+ years of experience building native iOS applications with Swift, SwiftUI, and UIKit.

My focus is on developing scalable features, improving existing apps, and writing clean, maintainable code. I have worked on products for startups, small businesses, and established media brands, including app modernization, UIKit-to-SwiftUI migration, reusable UI components, and App Store releases.

I am also open to collaborating with other developers on interesting iOS projects.
