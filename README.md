## About me

I am an iOS Engineer with 5+ years of experience building and maintaining native iOS applications using Swift, SwiftUI, and UIKit.

My primary focus is application architecture, scalable feature development, and long-term maintainability. I enjoy designing clean dependency boundaries, modernizing legacy codebases, and building production-ready user experiences that balance product requirements with engineering quality.

Over the years, I have worked on applications for startups, small businesses, and established media brands, contributing to feature development, UIKit-to-SwiftUI migrations, architecture refactoring, reusable design systems, subscription flows, and App Store releases.

Recently, I have been focusing on SwiftUI-first architectures, coordinator-driven navigation, SwiftData persistence, dependency injection, and protocol-oriented service design.

I am always interested in discussing iOS architecture, SwiftUI, and building high-quality mobile products.
